## Psychology of No Trade Days

> "The final piece of the puzzle is taking a trade. And that's the piece that didn't come together today."

**Temptation:**
- "I'm just gonna break the ice and trade this thing"
- "Even if it's not a perfect setup I might make money"

**Reality:**
- End of day = lighter liquidity
- Lower probability of success
- More likely to make mistake

---

## Decision Framework

```
If I take a trade and WIN:
  → Prep was worth it
  
If I take a trade and LOSE:
  → "You're a real idiot because there was 
     nothing good and instead of holding 
     discipline, you traded low quality"
```
