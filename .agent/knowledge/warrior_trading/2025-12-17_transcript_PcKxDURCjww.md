## Key Patterns Extracted

### Base Hits Strategy Philosophy (Critical)
1. Ross learned 2010-2019 when commissions existed
2. $10/trade meant less volume = less liquidity
3. Had to take profit at 15-20 cents/share
4. This held him back during pandemic (2020-2021)
5. But helped survive cool period (2022-2023)

### Pandemic Traders vs Pre-Pandemic Traders
| Pandemic Learners | Pre-Pandemic Learners |
|-------------------|----------------------|
| Buy and hold for $5 moves | In and out for 20 cents |
| Swing for home runs | Base hits, high accuracy |
| Worked great 2020-2021 | Worked in all markets |
| Blew up 2022-2023 | Survived drawdowns |

### Example: RADX Monday
- Ross in/out first trade at $5 for $8k
- Stock went to $28
- "Home run" traders made $150k on 10k shares
- Ross made $70k with in/out/in/out style
- Both valid, different risk profiles

### Float Doesn't Always Tell Truth
- ADTX shows 500k float
- Trades "thickly" like 5M float
- If it doesn't ACT like low float, it isn't
- Check price action, not just number
