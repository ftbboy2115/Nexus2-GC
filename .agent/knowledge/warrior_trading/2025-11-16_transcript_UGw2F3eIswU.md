## Small Account Strategy: One Trade or Many?

### The Dilemma
> "Should I focus on taking just one really good trade each day... or should I split up my buying power so I could take 5 or 8 or 10 trades?"

### Decision: One Trade Approach
**Pros:**
- Forces patience and quality focus
- Max capital on best opportunity
- No opportunity cost on A-setups

**Risk:**
- If first trade is red, must wait until tomorrow
- 70-80% accuracy means ~3 losses in 10 days

### Cash Account Math
```
Starting: $2,000
If lose 10% Day 1: $1,800
If lose 10% Day 2: $1,620
If win 10% Day 3: $1,782
Recovery takes longer!
```
