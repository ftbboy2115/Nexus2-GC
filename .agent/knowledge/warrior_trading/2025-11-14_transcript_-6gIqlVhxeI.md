## 5-Step Framework

### Step 1: Choose Your Market
- US equities = high liquidity + high volatility
- Billions of shares traded daily = easy buy/sell
- Stocks go up 20-100%+ daily on breaking news

### Step 2: Set Up Scanners
**Five Pillars of Stock Selection:**
1. Price: $2-$20 (focus on lower end for small account)
2. Relative Volume: 5x above average
3. Percentage Change: Already up 10%+
4. Float: Under 20M shares (under 5M in cold market)
5. Breaking News: Required catalyst

**Cold Market Adjustment:**
> "During a colder market, lower supply with even a medium amount of demand can still create a little bit of a move."

### Step 3: Candlestick Patterns
**Primary Pattern: Pullback Entry**
1. Wait for surge up (hits scanner)
2. Do due diligence during pullback
3. Confirm 5 pillars
4. Check MACD is positive
5. Buy first candle that makes new high
6. Target: retest of HOD

**MACD Gating:**
> "The MACD was the technical indicator that told me not to take this trade."

### Step 4: Trading Plan
- Window: 7 AM - 10 AM (historically best)
- Focus: Top 3 leading gainers
- Entry: First pullback when MACD positive
- Share size: Calculation based on price, buying power, 10% risk

### Step 5: Analyze Performance
**Critical Metrics:**
- Accuracy target: 75%
- Profit/Loss ratio: 2:1
- Review by: price range, hold time, float, rel volume, share size
