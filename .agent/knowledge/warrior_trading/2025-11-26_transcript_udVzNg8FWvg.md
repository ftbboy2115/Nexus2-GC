## CLSK - The "Obvious" Stock Problem

### Why It Was Difficult
```
Late morning pop (9 AM, nothing earlier)
  → Popped up, looked promising
  → $1.65 → $1.74 micro move
  → Sold for 3 cents/share × 30k = $1,000
  → But commissions eat into that
  → "Just wasn't really worth it"
```

### Price Action Issues
1. **Bull trap → Bear trap → Jack knife**
   - Squeezed into halt
   - Opened higher
   - Flushed down
   - Halted again
   - "Everyone loses"

2. **Massive Jack Knife**
   - Candle from $2.45 → $3.00 → $2.45
   - "Once a stock shows this kind of move, you can no longer trust it"

## Key Insight: When "Obvious" ≠ Tradeable

**Signs of Untradeable "Obvious" Stock:**

## Key Patterns Extracted

### Entry Rules
1. Relative volume 4,000x = attention (but not always tradeable)
2. Early morning setups preferred (nothing before 9 AM = concern)

### Exit Rules
1. 3 cents/share = not worth commission costs on direct access
2. Take what market gives, don't force range

### Avoid Rules
1. **Jack knife stocks** - once seen, trust is gone
2. **Bull/bear trap stocks** - "everyone loses"
3. **High volume but no range** - tug-of-war = no edge
4. **Trading during holiday weeks** - expectations low
