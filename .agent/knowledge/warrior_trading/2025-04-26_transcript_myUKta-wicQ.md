## Candlestick Anatomy

Every candlestick is created from **4 pieces of information**:
1. **Open** - Price at start of period
2. **High** - Highest price during period
3. **Low** - Lowest price during period
4. **Close** - Price at end of period

**Green candle**: Opened at bottom, closed at top (buyers won)
**Red candle**: Opened at top, closed at bottom (sellers won)

## Single Candle Patterns

### Long Body Candle
- **Definition**: Candle with large body, minimal wicks
- **Signal**: Strong sentiment (bullish if green, bearish if red)
- **Strongest pattern** — indicates momentum

### Short Body Candle
- **Definition**: Small body with small wicks
- **Signal**: Weaker sentiment than long body
- **Context**: Getting larger = momentum building; getting smaller = trend exhausting

### Doji
- **Definition**: Open and close at same price
- **Signal**: Indecision, potential reversal
- **At top of uptrend**: Bearish warning
- **At bottom of downtrend**: Bullish opportunity

### Gravestone Doji (BEARISH)
- **Definition**: Doji with long upper wick, no lower wick
- **Signal**: "Reversal has already begun" - price tried higher, rejected
- **Best at**: Top of uptrend

### Dragonfly Doji (BULLISH)
- **Definition**: Doji with long lower wick, no upper wick
- **Signal**: "Reversal has already begun" - price tried lower, rejected
- **Best at**: Bottom of downtrend
- **Entry**: "As soon as next candle turns green, even if only 3 seconds on it, I take my entry"

### Spinning Top
- **Definition**: Small body with relatively large upper and lower wicks
- **Signal**: Indecision, tug-of-war between buyers and sellers
- **Neutral** - doesn't indicate direction by itself

### Hammer (BULLISH)
- **Definition**: Small body at top, long lower wick
- **Signal**: "Hammering out a base" - sellers pushed down, buyers rallied back
- **Best at**: Bottom of downtrend
- **Entry**: First candle to make new high after hammer

### Hanging Man (BEARISH)
- **Definition**: Same shape as hammer, but at TOP of uptrend
- **Signal**: Bearish reversal warning
- **Context changes meaning**: Hammer at bottom = bullish; same shape at top = bearish

### Inverted Hammer
- **Definition**: Small body at bottom, long upper wick
- **At bottom**: Not ideal (tried higher, failed)
- **At top (Shooting Star)**: Strong bearish signal

### Shooting Star (BEARISH)
- **Definition**: Same as inverted hammer, but at TOP of uptrend
- **Signal**: Strong reversal indicator - "shooting stars come back down to earth"
- **Worse than hanging man** - very bearish

## Two Candle Patterns

### Tweezer Top (BEARISH)
- **Definition**: Two candles with matching upper wicks at top of trend
- **Signal**: Double rejection at resistance = reversal coming

### Tweezer Bottom (BULLISH)
- **Definition**: Two candles with matching lower wicks at bottom of trend
- **Signal**: Double bottom = strong support, reversal likely

### Bullish Engulfing
- **Definition**: Green candle fully engulfs previous red candle
- **Signal**: Very strong bullish reversal
- **Note**: Common on daily charts (requires gap)

### Bearish Engulfing
- **Definition**: Red candle fully engulfs previous green candle
- **Signal**: Very strong bearish reversal

## Three Candle Patterns

### Morning Star (BULLISH)
- **Definition**: Downtrend → gap down with hammer/doji → rally
- **Signal**: Daily chart reversal pattern
- **Entry timing**: "Between 3 and 4 PM when daily candle is about to close"

### Evening Star (BEARISH)
- **Definition**: Uptrend → gap up with shooting star/doji → selloff
- **Signal**: Daily chart reversal pattern

### Three White Soldiers
- **Definition**: Three large green candles in a row
- **Signal**: Very bullish momentum

### Three Black Crows
- **Definition**: Three large red candles in a row
- **Signal**: Very bearish momentum (strong selling pressure)

### Rising Three (Bull Flag)
- **Definition**: Green candle(s) → 3 pullback candles → continuation higher
- **Signal**: Consolidation before next leg up
- **Entry**: "First candle to make a new high"
- **Stop**: "Low of the pullback"

### Falling Three (Bear Flag)
- **Definition**: Red candle(s) → 3 bounce candles → continuation lower
- **Signal**: Consolidation before next leg down

## Key Trading Rules Extracted

### Entry Rules
1. **First candle to make a new high** - Primary entry trigger after pullback
2. **Wait for confirmation** - "Don't get in too early, wait for first candle to go green"
3. **Dragonfly doji entry** - "As soon as next candle turns green, even if only 3 seconds, I take entry"

### Stop Loss Rules
1. **Low of pullback pattern** - "When doing pullback trades, stop at low of the pullback"
2. **Max loss at pattern low** - "Max loss is at the low of this pattern"

### Profit Target Rules
1. **Retest of high of day** - "Profit target is a move back to the high of day"
2. **Logical targets** - Previous resistance levels

### Context Rules
1. **Patterns require context** - Individual candles meaningless without bigger picture
2. **Position matters** - Same shape means different things at top vs bottom
3. **Trend exhaustion** - Watch for candles getting smaller = momentum fading
4. **Sideways action** - Patterns during consolidation are less significant

### Stock Selection (Trading Right Instrument)
1. Trade **leading percentage gainer** each day
2. Focus on **lower priced stocks** ($2-10 easier to move than $40)
3. Require **breaking news/catalyst**
4. Need **high volume** for good pattern resolution
