## Key Patterns Extracted

### Short Squeeze Mechanics
1. No news + big move = shorts trap themselves
2. Each pop that doesn't fade attracts more short sellers
3. Eventually shorts forced to cover = explosive move
4. Company without shelf registration = less dilution risk

### Entry Rules
1. Break ice with small trades to gauge action
2. Build cushion ($15k+) before sizing up
3. 20k share position only AFTER cushion established
4. Buy into halt going up (gap potential)

### Halt Trading Rules
1. Gap up resumption = bullish, hold
2. Flat resumption = sentiment shift, caution
3. Gap down resumption = bearish, exit
4. Don't buy going into halt with only 2 cents profit

### After-Hours Avoidance (Critical)
1. Spreads of $5-$10 make after-hours untradeable
2. Bids disappear instantly, algos pull orders
3. Volume declining = less liquidity to exit
4. "It's a mirage - not real profit potential"

### Avoid Rules
1. Don't trade after-hours on wide spread stocks
2. Don't expect to trade SMX-style moves ($13 spreads)
3. Prefer stocks <$10 for risk management
4. Focus on pre-market 7am window, not 4pm
