## When to Use Which Broker

### Commission-Free (Webull)
✅ Small account (<$25k)
✅ Small position sizes
✅ Quick scalps in/out
✅ Break-even trades
✅ Price improvement helps

### Direct Access (Lightspeed)
✅ Hot market with big moves
✅ Large position sizes (20k+ shares)
✅ When you need speed over savings
✅ Account >$25k

### The Crossover Point
> "With a tiny account, you start benefiting from price improvement. With direct access, you're actually losing money until you can take bigger size."
