## Reverse Split Chart Mechanics

> "It looks like it had a high of $500 million a share. But it never actually traded at that level."

**How Reverse Splits Stack:**
```
Real high: ~$50
10:1 split → chart shows $500
Another 10:1 → chart shows $5,000
Another 10:1 → chart shows $50,000
...and so on
```

**Why Shorts Have Bias:**
- Stock keeps going lower
- Company does split so it can't hit zero
- Short from $2,400 (adjusted) → currently $70
- Understanding why shorts are stubborn = edge
