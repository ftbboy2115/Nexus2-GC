## The "Less Is More" Philosophy (CRITICAL)

### The Statistical Proof
> "Last year I took 40% fewer trades than 2024 and made 3x as much money."

### How It Works
```
Fewer trades
  → Being patient with choppy markets
  → Being patient with stocks I didn't love
  → Fewer losers
  → Higher accuracy
  → More confidence
  → Bigger positions
  → More profit
```

### The Cycle of Trading
Ross describes these market phases:
1. **Hot streak**: Back-to-back big green days
2. **Mixed**: Good trades one day, no trades the next
3. **Cold streak**: Very few trades, hard to be patient

### The Discipline Challenge
> "I'm sitting here with a job to do. If I don't take at least one trade, I haven't done my job. But then I ask myself, would I rather have a no trade day or be red?"
