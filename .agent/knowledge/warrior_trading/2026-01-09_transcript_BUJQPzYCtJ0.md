## Key Patterns Extracted

### Entry Rules (What Went Wrong)
1. **Don't chase extended stocks** [PARAPHRASED]: Enter on the initial breakout, not after the stock has already made a big move
2. **VWAP must hold** [PARAPHRASED]: If a stock can't hold above VWAP, it's a warning sign

### Exit Rules
1. **Cut losses quickly** [PARAPHRASED]: "I didn't want to turn a small loss into a big loss"
2. **Defined stop at entry candle low** [PARAPHRASED]: Uses the low of the entry candle or breakout candle as the stop

### Risk Management (Red Day Discipline)
1. **Reduce size after a loss** [PARAPHRASED]: After losing on QUBT, Ross reduced his position size on NKLA
2. **Don't try to make it all back** [PARAPHRASED]: Ross chose to take a small total loss rather than increasing risk to recover
3. **Walk away when it's not working** [PARAPHRASED]: After two losses, Ross stopped trading for the day

### Re-entry
1. **No re-entry on red days** [PARAPHRASED]: Ross specifically chose NOT to re-enter after losses, recognizing the emotional and financial risk of trying to recover during a bad session
