## MASTERCLASS: Hidden Buyers & Sellers

### Definition
- **Hidden Buyer:** Large buy orders not displayed on Level 2, but visible through price action (price won't drop despite selling)
- **Hidden Seller:** Large sell orders not displayed, but visible when price won't break despite buying (iceberg order)

### The Dam Analogy
> "Think of a dam. The seller is that dam. If that seller breaks, the dam is going to let loose and this thing is going to squeeze."

### How to Spot Them
1. Watch time & sales for order flow
2. Compare to displayed Level 2 orders
3. If more buy orders go through than sellers displayed → hidden seller
4. If more sell orders go through but price doesn't drop → hidden buyer

### Where They Appear
- **Whole dollars** ($12, $13, $14) - psychological levels
- **Half dollars** ($12.50)
- **Support/resistance zones**
- **Most common on:** Leading gainers with 5x+ relative volume
