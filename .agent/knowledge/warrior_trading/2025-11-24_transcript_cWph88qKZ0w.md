## Key Patterns Extracted

### Entry Rules
1. Acquisition headlines = strong catalyst (GLTO precedent)

3. Wait for scanner alert, don't anticipate

### Exit Rules
1. Take profit at first resistance break
2. Don't turn winners into losers
3. "Pay yourself" mentality from trench days

### Avoid Rules
1. **Holding for bigger move** - in trenches mode
2. **Chinese stocks** - EPSM, JXG both ignored despite moves
3. **ALGO spikes that unwind** - JXG 4 AM spike sold off
