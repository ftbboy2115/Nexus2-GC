## BULLISH PATTERNS

### Bull Flag Breakout Pattern
**Entry Rule**: "Price above this candle's high is considered a buy, the close of the next candle would be confirmation of an entry."
- Look for tight consolidation after initial surge
- Use 5-min chart to spot pattern, then 1-min for precision entry
- Next candle closed above previous candle's high = confirmation

### Red to Green Move
**Entry**: Enter at 9:30am EST when price moves from red (pre-market) to green
- Pre-market shows choppy red candles
- Entry point is when first green candle appears after market open

### VWAP Breakout
- Entry when price breaks above VWAP with volume
- Strong momentum continuation signal

### First Pullback after Break of VWAP
- Buy the first pullback after initial VWAP break
- Wait for price to consolidate above VWAP, then break higher

### Micro Pullback before 50% Squeeze
- Small consolidation before major squeeze move
- Tight risk with large reward potential

### Continuation and Multi-Day Parabolic Momentum
- Stocks making sustained multi-day runs
- Look for continuation patterns on daily charts

### Whole Dollar & Half Dollar Entries
**Entry Rule**: "Buy first candle to break the .00 or .50"
- Psychological levels act as breakout triggers
- Clean entry points at round numbers

## BEARISH PATTERNS (For Shorting)

### Bear Flag Breakdown Pattern (1-min)
- Mirror of bull flag for shorting
- Tight consolidation after initial drop
- Entry on break below flag support

### Flat Bottom Breakdown Pattern
**Example**: "Short @ 55.25 with stop @ 55.51 - Covered on move down to 52.34"
- Clean horizontal support breaks down
- Tight stop above breakdown level

### Moving Average Pop (Shorting)
- Price fails at moving average resistance
- Good short opportunity on rejection

### VWAP Fade
- Short when price fails to hold above VWAP
- Fading the VWAP break failure

### Break Below Moving Average
- Bearish signal when price breaks key MA
- Trend shift confirmation

### Shift in Trend Entry
- Pattern showing 1-2-3 reversal structure
- Entry options on breakdown confirmation

### Head and Shoulders
- Classic reversal pattern
- Left shoulder, head, right shoulder formation
- Entry on neckline break

## RESISTANCE & SUPPORT

### Support/Resistance (Ascending)
- Rising support with horizontal resistance
- Triangle-like compression pattern

### Resistance Breakout
**Example**: "Resistance at 4.00 then Finally a Breakout"
- Multiple tests of resistance level
- Entry when clean breakout occurs

---

## WARNING PATTERNS (What to Avoid)

### Bull Flag Trap
- Pattern looks like bull flag but fails
- Breakdown instead of breakout
- Warning: not all consolidations work

### Buyout Headline that Ends up Not Being Real
- Fake news catalyst causes spike and collapse
- Extreme caution with headline-based moves

### Bad Pre-Market Chart
- Extended/overextended pre-market action
- Usually fails to follow through at open

### ABCD Flag Pattern that Stalled Out
- Pattern setup that didn't complete
- Example of when to recognize failure
