### Entry Rules
1. Micro-pullback entries on momentum stocks
2. Recognize spoofer patterns (large sell orders that move away from market = short seller getting squeezed)

### Add Rules

2. Multiple scalps: green/green/green/red pattern acceptable
3. Don't add too high after extended move (ADVB lesson)

### Exit Rules
1. Take profit into pops during squeeze
2. 30% giveback from peak = stop trading for the day
3. Scale out, don't hold for full reversal

### Avoid Rules
1. Don't cap green days — keep trading while odds favor you

3. Hard stop: 50% giveback = mandatory walk away
