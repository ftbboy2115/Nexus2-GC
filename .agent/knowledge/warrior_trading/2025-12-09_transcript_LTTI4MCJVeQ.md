## Key Patterns Extracted

### Drawdown Recovery Protocol
1. Day 1: Focus on not losing, not on making back
2. Start with smaller size than normal
3. Don't trade P/L, trade the market
4. Profit is byproduct of process, not goal

### Recovery Mindset
- "If you came in trying to make $38k back, you'd trade from desperation"
- "Trading your P/L, not the market = recipe for bigger loss"
- "$10k max risk today" = clear boundary

### SPAC Trading Notes
1. SPACs = shell companies that merge with private companies
2. Merger announcement = catalyst
3. Often light volume (500k-750k shares)
4. NASDAQ can halt for "more information"

### Tweezer Top Recognition
1. Two consecutive topping tails
2. Price rejected at same level twice
3. Bearish signal = exit position
4. Don't hold hoping for continuation
