## Key Patterns Extracted

### Profile Building Process
1. After big mover (SMX, TGL), identify common characteristics
2. Create scanner for similar stocks
3. Check short interest via Ortex
4. Verify float accuracy (foreign = risk of inaccuracy)

### Foreign Company Float Warning
- Some foreign companies secretly issue more shares
- Float may not be accurate on Chinese/HK/Singapore stocks
- If not trading like low-float, it probably isn't
- US/Australian/European/Canadian floats more reliable

### Theme Cycle
1. Theme emerges (reverse splits + short squeezes)
2. Traders pile in
3. Theme can exhaust quickly
4. Watch for next theme to emerge

### Monday Morning Game Plan
1. Check scanners at 6:45 AM for pre-market
2. Look for reverse splits moving early
3. If nothing by 7 AM, wait for fresh breakout
4. Don't chase after-hours movers into the open
