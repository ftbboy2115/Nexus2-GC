### Add Rules
1. Add into the squeeze after initial confirmation
2. Add when stock "curls back up" after dip
3. Add on breakout through resistance levels ($8.50, $9.50)

### Exit Rules
1. Take half off at new highs
2. Walk away when you give back profit - "mission accomplished"
3. Don't overstay welcome - "not overstay my welcome"

2. Skip "grinders" - thickly traded, slow despite volume
3. **Chinese/HK stocks** - can't trade at Schwab (need backup broker)
4. If nothing sets up by 9:30am, switch gears
