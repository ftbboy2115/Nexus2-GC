## Key Educational Content: Cold Market Short Squeeze Dynamics

### The Short Seller Confidence Trap
> "In order for shorts to make money, they're shorting earlier and earlier so they can profit on the smaller range."

**Cold Market Pattern:**
1. Shorts get confident - everything fading
2. They short earlier and add more aggressively
3. Range compresses as shorts suppress moves
4. UNTIL one stock holds and squeezes
5. Then shorts get trapped → squeeze

## Key Patterns Extracted

### Entry Rules
1. First trade of day = smallest size (building cushion)
2. Leading gainers get most volume → focus there
3. Penny stocks can work in cold market (low float advantage)

### Exit Rules
1. Take profits during halt-up sequences
2. Don't add at extended levels after big move

### Avoid Rules
1. **First trade full-size** - build cushion first
2. **Adding after extended move** - gave back gains on FTEEL
3. **Fighting the cold market** - accept smaller wins
