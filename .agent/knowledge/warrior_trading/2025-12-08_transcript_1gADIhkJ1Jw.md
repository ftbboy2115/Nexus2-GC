### Red Day Response Rules
1. After first big loss, reduce size dramatically
2. Don't chase to recover losses same day
3. Accept the day is a loss, preserve capital for tomorrow
4. Shut down screens, don't look at charts rest of day

### Avoid Rules

2. Don't add to losers hoping for recovery
3. Don't hold winners until they become losers
4. Don't fight the market when action is choppy
