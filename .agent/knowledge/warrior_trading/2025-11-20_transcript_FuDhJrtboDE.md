## Key Patterns Extracted

### Entry Rules
1. Sub-1M float with news = potential squeeze breaker
2. Red flame = breaking news just hit
3. Meets 4 of 5 pillars = worth fast investigation

### Exit Rules
1. Take profit at first resistance (small account priority)
2. Don't hold hoping - especially Chinese stocks

### Avoid Rules
1. **Chinese stocks in current climate** - unreliable float data, dump patterns
2. **Chasing in big account after small account entry** - different risk profile
3. **Trading both accounts simultaneously** - "not easy"
