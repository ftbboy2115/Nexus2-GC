### Add Rules
1. Re-enter on bounces off ascending support trendline
2. Add when breaking above key resistance levels ($28.99, $29.30)

### Exit Rules
1. Take profits into strength on each push
2. Stop trading when stock can't push to new highs

### Avoid Rules
1. Don't trade stocks that squeeze BEFORE news comes out — suspected insider activity
2. Don't trade stocks gapping on no news with lighter volume (MLEC)
